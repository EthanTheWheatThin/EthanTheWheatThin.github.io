All About ATM

What is ATM?

Bancroft Middle School's Applied Techonology Magnet or also known as ATM is a 3 years Computer Coding/Programming elective course. The 3 year program begins in 6th grade and continues every year through 8th grade. The goal of the program is when the student finishes the program, they will have technical skills and will have experience with professional softare that will prepare them for more advanced programs in high school, college, and future careers.

What Do You Learn?

Much of your time in the ATM Program will be you interacting and using a wide selection of computer programs. In the ATM program your grade (6-8) determinds what type of programs you will be using. In 6th grade you learn the basics about computers in general and how to make spreadsheets and other basic computer skills. In 7th grade, Students will use appropriate terminology related to hardware and software throughout the semester. They will continue to deal with legal, social and ethical issues related to the use of computers in our daily life. Lastly, in 8th grade you will learn about the fun part of coding. You will make your own websites and will also have enough coding skills to be able to code anything you can think of. Here are the computer programs that you will work with in the ATM program:

  • Sketchup
  • Floorplanner
  • Illustrator
  • Microftsoft Excel, Word, and Powerpoint
  • Khan Academy Basic Coding
  • HTML and CSS coding
  • Khan Academy Advanced Coding
  • Visual Studio Code
