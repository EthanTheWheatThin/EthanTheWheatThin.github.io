The Rangers

What Are Army Rangers?

A U.S. Army Ranger is a member of an elite force that conducts special military missions on short notice. The Ranger Regiment is the largest special operations combat group in the Army. Members of this regiment have combat and airborne training, and they also hold varied occupations in the Army.

Michael Browning

Triaing To Become a Ranger

Army Ranger Training. Like all Ranger candidates you will begin with nine weeks of Boot Camp. Upon completion of Basic Training you will attend Advanced Individual Training to earn your Military Occupational Specialty. This training varies in length depending on your selected Military Occupational Specialty.

Mike Throm
